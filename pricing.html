Questions, answered plainly

What do you need from me?

Thirty minutes on a call, plus whatever you have: photos, a logo, old brochure copy, your hours and services. We turn that into the site. If all you have is a business name, that works too.

I don't have photos or a logo. Is that a problem?

No. We source licensed photography that fits your trade, and a clean typographic logo is included in every build. Full logo design is part of the Site + brand package.

Who owns the website?

You do, completely: the files, the domain, the hosting account, the images. If we part ways tomorrow, everything keeps working and everything is yours.

What does it cost after launch?

Your domain, about $23 a year. Hosting for sites like these is free on modern platforms, set up in your name. The optional $50/month care plan covers management plus edits whenever you want them.

What if I want changes later?

Two revision rounds are included before launch. After that, small text and photo swaps are covered by the care plan, and bigger changes get a flat quote in writing before any work starts.

Why is this so much cheaper than an agency?

Fixed scope, modern tooling, and no meetings about meetings. We build one site at a time in 72 focused hours, without an office to pay for. The price is the honest cost of the work.
