Forty-two seats and a hearth

Everything that leaves this kitchen touches the wood fire: oak for the steaks, fruit woods for fish and vegetables. The menu changes when the farmers market does, which is roughly every three weeks.

Half the room is bookable. The other half, including the counter in front of the hearth, is kept for walk-ins.

The kitchen

Chef Marisol Vega cooked eight years at hearth-driven rooms in Los Angeles before coming home to Riverside to open her own. The brigade is small, the prep list is long, and nothing is finished anywhere but over the fire.

"Short menus keep us honest. If it's on the page, it's because it's good this week."Marisol Vega, chef and owner

Private dining for 12 to 40

The back room seats twelve at one table. Buy out the whole floor for forty. Set menus, wine pairings, and one point of contact who answers the same day.
