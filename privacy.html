Privacy policy

Last updated July 2026

What we collect

If you use the contact form, we receive what you type: your name, email, phone number if you add it, your business name, and your notes. That's the only personal information this site collects.

What we do with it

We use it to reply to you about your project. Nothing else. We don't sell it, rent it, share it with advertisers, or add you to a mailing list.

How it's processed

Form submissions are delivered to our email inbox by a form-delivery service, and then live in our email like any correspondence. This site sets no cookies and runs no analytics or tracking scripts.

Deletion

Want your information deleted? Email us at the address in the footer of the main page and we'll delete the correspondence and confirm within a few days.

Changes

If this policy changes, the date above changes with it.
